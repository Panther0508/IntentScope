Import the repository in the Vercel dashboard or use the CLI:\n   ```bash\n   npm i -g vercel\n   vercel --prod\n   ```\n\n2. After deployment, your site will be available at `https://intentscope.vercel.app` (or a custom domain you configure).\n\n## Environment Variables\n\nNo secrets required – the app is fully client-side.
